District level inauguration of e-Office system in Taluks and RDO's

The new version of e-Office(eFile 7.2.0) has been launched at District Collectorate Alappuzha.Also One day Training Programme on e-Office new version for the staffs was held on the 22nd of February 2022,The programme was led by Sri.Nikesh Poovathinchal from KSITM.
